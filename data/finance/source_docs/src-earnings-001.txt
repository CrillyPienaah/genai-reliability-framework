Bank Earnings Reporting — Q3 2025 Sample Reference

SAMPLE CANADIAN BANK Q3 2025 EARNINGS DATA (ILLUSTRATIVE):

Net Income: $3.8 billion (reported net income attributable to common shareholders)
Revenue (Total): $14.2 billion
Net Interest Income: $8.9 billion
Non-Interest Income: $5.3 billion
Provision for Credit Losses: $1.1 billion
Non-Interest Expenses: $7.8 billion
Efficiency Ratio: 54.9%
CET1 Ratio: 13.2%
Return on Equity: 14.7%
Diluted EPS: $2.64

ACCURACY REQUIREMENTS FOR LLM SUMMARIES:
All numerical figures in AI-generated earnings summaries must exactly match the reported figures. A net income figure of $4.2 billion when the actual reported figure is $3.8 billion represents a $400 million (10.5%) overstatement. This is not an acceptable margin of error in financial reporting.

Consequences of inaccurate AI-generated earnings summaries:
- Investor misinformation leading to incorrect investment decisions
- Regulatory violations under securities disclosure rules
- Reputational damage to the bank and the AI system provider
- Potential liability under securities legislation

LLM outputs containing financial figures must be validated against source documents (press releases, MD&A, financial statements) before use.
