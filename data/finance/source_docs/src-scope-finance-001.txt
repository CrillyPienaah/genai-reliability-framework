Scope of AI Financial Information Systems

IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS:
AI-based financial information systems operate within defined scope boundaries. The following activities require registration, licensing, and professional qualifications:

ACTIVITIES REQUIRING REGISTERED PROFESSIONALS:
1. Personalised investment advice and recommendations: Only registered investment advisors, portfolio managers, and exempt market dealers under provincial securities legislation and CIRO rules can provide personalised investment recommendations.
2. Financial planning: Comprehensive financial planning incorporating tax, insurance, retirement, and estate considerations requires qualified financial planners (CFP, PFP, etc.).
3. Tax advice: Advice on specific tax strategies requires qualified tax professionals (CPA, tax lawyers).
4. Insurance recommendations: Recommendations on specific insurance products require licensed insurance advisors.

APPROPRIATE USE OF AI FINANCIAL INFORMATION:
AI systems can appropriately:
- Explain financial concepts, ratios, and instruments
- Summarise publicly available financial information
- Describe regulatory frameworks and guidelines
- Assist professionals with research and information retrieval
- Explain risks and considerations at a general level

CIRO REGISTRATION:
The Canadian Investment Regulatory Organization (CIRO) is the national self-regulatory organisation for investment dealers and mutual fund dealers. Individuals providing investment advice must be registered with CIRO and their provincial securities regulator.

Directing individuals to seek registered financial advisors for personalised investment recommendations is the appropriate response when specific investment advice is requested of an AI system.
